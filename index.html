Helping Wild Animals in Humboldt County

Every wild life deserves a second chance.

When a hummingbird strikes a window, when an owl is poisoned, when a young raccoon loses its mother—we answer the call. Treating over 1,200 injured wild animals each year in California's northwest corner.

What We Do

Wildlife Rescue & Rehabilitation

From sunrise rescues to late-night emergencies, our team provides expert medical care to birds, mammals, and other wildlife caught in unfortunate encounters with human activity.

Learn More →

Humane Solutions

Not every wildlife encounter requires rescue. Our team helps resolve conflicts between people and wildlife—like raccoons nesting in attics—keeping wild families together.

Professional Training & Education

We elevate wildlife care standards everywhere through workshops, publications, and consultations, sharing expertise with rehabilitators across the country.
